## Structural tells

- Three or more consecutive paragraphs with nearly identical length
- Every H2 starts with "Why" or "How to" or "What is"
- Lists of exactly five items with parallel empty grammar
- Round percentages (73%, 85%) with no named source nearby

## Scan rule

- 0 hits: pass
- 1 hit: warning (rewrite before publish)
- 2+ hits: fail

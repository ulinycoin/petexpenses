Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2026 · Last updated: June 2026

petexpenses.com (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. What We Collect

Information you provide

You do not need an account to use our calculator. The only information you voluntarily provide is through email correspondence (e.g., contact@petexpenses.com). We do not require registration, newsletter sign-ups, or user profiles.

Information we collect automatically

We use PostHog (self-hosted on EU servers) to collect anonymised usage data:

  • Page views and feature interactions (e.g., calculator button clicks, share events)
  • Browser type, device type, and approximate geographic region (country-level only)
  • Referral source (how you found the site)

PostHog is configured to not collect IP addresses. User IDs are anonymous, random strings stored in your browser’s localStorage — not linked to any personal identifier.

Affiliate links

Clicking an affiliate link (e.g., through CJ Affiliate) may set a tracking cookie from the partner network. These cookies are set by the affiliate network, not by us, and we do not have access to them. You can disable cookie tracking in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Data

  • To improve the calculator and prioritise features (which breeds to add, which tools to build next)
  • To detect technical issues (broken buttons, slow pages, null states)
  • To understand which articles and comparisons are most helpful to visitors

We do not sell your data. We do not share usage data with third parties. We do not show personalised ads based on browsing behaviour.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

Our calculator stores your inputs (breed, weight, age, activity level, diet) in your browser’s localStorage. This data never leaves your device. We use no third-party cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

Affiliate network cookies (see Section 1) are outside our control. These are typically session cookies that expire within 30 days.

5. Data Retention

PostHog event data is retained for 12 months. After that, aggregate statistics may be kept, but individual events are permanently deleted. localStorage data is retained until you clear your browser storage.

6. Your Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request removal of your data from our analytics
  • Opt out — you can block analytics cookies using browser extensions (e.g., uBlock Origin) or by disabling JavaScript

To exercise these rights, email contact@petexpenses.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
